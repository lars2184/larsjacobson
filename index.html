Is your site mobile friendly?

Google recently made a change that boosts mobile friendly web sites. If your web site isn’t easy to use on a mobile device, not only is it harder for customers to view your content, it’s harder for customers to find your site to begin with. To test if your site is mobile friendly, use Google’s site analyzer tool. If your site doesn’t pass, I can help you update your site so it does.
